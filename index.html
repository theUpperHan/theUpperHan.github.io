Biography

In brief, I am studying in Game Design and Development Major under Purdue Polytechnic Institute also I am minoring in Computer Science in Purdue University, and I am most interested in the back-end development and programming segment of game companies.

Speak of the decision that I have made to become one of the students in game development, I have to say that it is a fortunate coincidence. Since I was a kid, I was into games with my brother and back in 2001, the video game consoles like Xbox and PS are not as popular as today, and the popular games are mostly on PCs. The video games back in the 2000s were seen as the negative influences on the young generations. Luckily, my parents do not hold strong hostile attitude towards the games, and they allowed us play video games if we have earned good grades and finish our homework on time. Sometimes, I will go out with my friends to play Street Fighter on arcades. As I played more and more games, I get to know the games that I have played early are developed by different game companies, for examples, the Armor Games, Newgrounds, Capcom, etc. As soon as I know that there are people making game development as their career, I made the promise to myself that someday I will make a game that can bring fun to people. The time comes to my senior year of high school, I received two college offers that I am mostly interested in, one is a computer science major and the other one is the game development major came from Purdue University. My parents hope that I can choose the computer science offer at first, since it was a more popular and more familiar major, but I think that I will still learn programming skills, but I would love to use these skills on creating somethings that I am truly enthusiastic about, like video games.
